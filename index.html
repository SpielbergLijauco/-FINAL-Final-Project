Welcome to SM Corporation

As one of the leading retail and lifestyle chains in Southeast Asia, SM Supermalls brings world-class shopping, dining, and entertainment experiences to millions of Filipinos nationwide. Our malls serve as vibrant community centers—where families, friends, and businesses come together to create lasting memories.

With over 80 malls across the Philippines and several in China, SM continues to redefine the malling experience through innovation, customer service, and a commitment to sustainability and community development.

Whether you’re here to explore our services, discover upcoming events, or learn more about our history and vision, you’ve come to the right place. At SM, our mission goes beyond retail. We aim to uplift communities, support local industries, and make everyday living more meaningful.
