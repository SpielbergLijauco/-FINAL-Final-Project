About SM Malls

SM Malls is one of the largest shopping mall chains in the Philippines, offering a wide range of local and international brands, dining experiences, entertainment options, and more. We are committed to delivering the best mall experience for every Filipino family.

SM Supermalls aims to become Asia’s most popular mall chain through constant innovation and partnership with the world’s best retailers. We provide the family fun mall experience that makes every day better for all. Together with our partners and employees, we commit to a better quality of life for the communities we serve by supporting programs and activities that are socially responsible and environmentally sustainable.

SM Supermalls is one of Southeast Asia’s biggest mall chains, known for offering world-class shopping, dining, and entertainment experiences. Part of the SM Group founded by Henry Sy Sr., SM has become a household name in the Philippines and beyond.

The journey started in 1958 with a humble shoe store called Shoemart in Manila. Over time, it evolved into a retail empire, with the first SM Supermall opening in 1985 — SM City North EDSA. Today, there are over 80 SM malls across the Philippines and several overseas, drawing millions of visitors weekly.

What sets SM apart is its unique blend of affordability, accessibility, and scale. SM Malls are more than just shopping centers — they serve as community hubs offering convenience, safety, and family-friendly spaces. With iconic locations like SM Mall of Asia, SM Aura, and SM Megamall, SM continues to lead the way in redefining the Filipino mall experience.
